Ovaj projekat istražuje primenu različitih metoda mašinskog učenja za predviđanje popularnosti onlajn članaka na osnovu skupa podataka Online News Popularity. Fokus rada je na transformaciji sirovih podataka kroz napredne tehnike čišćenja, logaritamsko skaliranje i kreiranje novih indikatora (feature engineering) koji bolje oslikavaju faktore viralnosti vesti. Kroz poređenje linearnih modela (Ridge, Lasso) sa kompleksnijim algoritmima poput stabala odlučivanja, Random Forest-a i optimizovanog XGBoost-a, projekat nudi uvid u to koji pristupi daju najpreciznije procene broja deljenja (shares) u digitalnom ekosistemu.
This project explores the application of various machine learning methods to predict the popularity of online articles based on the Online News Popularity dataset. The core of the work involves transforming raw data using advanced cleaning techniques, logarithmic scaling, and feature engineering to create new indicators that better capture the drivers of news virality. By comparing linear models (Ridge, Lasso) with more complex algorithms such as Decision Trees, Random Forest, and optimized XGBoost, the project provides insight into which approaches yield the most accurate estimates of article shares in a digital environment.
